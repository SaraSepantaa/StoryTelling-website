Jahan Ba Man Beraghs: A Journey Through Life and Dance

"Jahan Ba Man Beraghs" captures the essence of life’s fleeting beauty through the lens of friendship, mortality, and the joy of living.

Project Purpose

"Jahan Ba Man Beraghs" is more than a film; it is a heartfelt reminder that life, with all its fleeting moments and imperfections, can still be beautiful and meaningful. This story, set against the backdrop of humor and sorrow, invites us to cherish the relationships and moments that truly matter. Jahangir, despite facing his final days, teaches us that life’s value lies not in its length but in the depth of the connections we make and the joy we share.

This project seeks to explore the profound message of the film: how can we embrace life even when faced with its inevitable end?

"We dance—briefly, fast, or slow—each in our own way. But it’s this dance that can immortalize us in the great hall of life."
Sara Skandari
