Likely Influences and Connections

About Memento

  • Both deal with non-linear and ambiguous storytelling.
  • Both explore the unpredictability of life and existential questions.

About Life of Pi

  • Journeys in both films lead to a remembrance of meaning acquired through storytelling.
  • Symbolism: The villa in "Jahan Ba Man Beraghs"; the zoo animals in Life of Pi.

Connections with Folklore/Mythology

  • The journey to the villa is reminiscent of a transforming myth.
  • In such myths, trials bequeath wisdom to the characters.

Memento Mori

  • Both films share a death theme.
  • They reinforce reflection and the acceptance of life's end.

Cultural and Social Relevance

Cultural implication: The film portrays Iranian communal values and the ethics of summaries and reconciling in a community.

The villa: Set up as a retreat for reflection, aligned with Iranian traditions for clearing the mind.

Societal significance: Universal themes of mortality and purpose provide space for reconciling differences in individual relationships and addressing contemporary social complexities.
